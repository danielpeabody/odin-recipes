Air Fryer Hamburger Patties

Description

Everyone loves a good hamburger right!
This hamburger recipe is perfect for
those people who are in a hurry and need
somthing quick that tastes great!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 ounce of grated cheese
  • 1 tablespoon French-fried onions
  • 1 teaspoon dry ranch dressing mix
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat airfryer to 370 degrees
  2. Place Ingredients into a bowl and mix
  3. Air fry until temp is 160
