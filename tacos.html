Tacos

Description

Your going to love these new beef tacos.
This recipe is super quick and easy,
perfect for those big family meals.
Follow the recipe below and let us know what you think.

Ingredients

  • 18 medium taco shellls
  • 2 pounds of lean ground beef
  • 1 bottle of ketchup
  • 1 package shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 large tomato,diced
  • 1 iceberg lettuce,shredded

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Warm taco shells for 5 minutes
  3. Cook beef in a pan
  4. Add it all together in a taco shell!
