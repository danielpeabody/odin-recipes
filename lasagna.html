Lasagna

Description

Grammy's Overnight Lasagna has always
has always been a favorite here and
we're sure you'll love it too. There
is nothing quite like a homemade lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 package cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Arrange noodles on baking dish
  3. Spoon 1/2 of the sauce on noodles
  4. Top the noodles with the cheese
  5. Cover with foil
  6. Heat oven to 350
  7. Bake lasagna for 1 hour
